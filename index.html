What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AEO is the practice of optimizing your practice to appear in AI-generated responses from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot. When potential clients ask these tools "What is the best Med Spa near me?" or "Where can I get Botox in [city]?" — AEO ensures your practice gets cited. It's the next frontier of search, and most Med Spas aren't on it yet.

How long does it take to get a new website live?

Approximately one week from signed contract to live site. We use AI-assisted workflows to dramatically accelerate the build process while maintaining quality. Compare that to the 4–8 week industry standard. You start showing up in search while your competitors are still waiting for their agency to finish.

Do you work with Med Spas of all sizes?

Yes. We work with single-location owner-operators, multi-location practices, and everything in between. Our Phase 1 focus is on single-location owner-operators where we can deliver the fastest impact — these practices see the biggest lift from done-right digital marketing.
