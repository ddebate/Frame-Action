Why Reviews Matter

Reviews Are Your Most Powerful Marketing Asset

Med Spa clients make decisions based on social proof. A strong Google review profile drives both new bookings and local SEO rankings — it's one of the highest-ROI investments you can make.

Review Generation

Proactive systems that prompt happy clients to leave Google reviews at the right moment — post-treatment, via SMS or email — dramatically increasing your review volume without being pushy.

Review Monitoring

Real-time alerts for new reviews across Google and other platforms. Never miss a review — positive or negative — so you can respond promptly and professionally.

Response Strategy

Professional response templates and active management of your review responses. Every review — 5-star or 1-star — gets a thoughtful, brand-consistent reply that builds trust with future clients reading them.

The Impact

Reviews Affect Everything

Online reviews aren't just social proof — they're a significant ranking factor for local SEO. More high-quality reviews mean better map pack rankings, which means more visibility, more traffic, and more bookings.

Better Google Rankings

Google factors your review volume, recency, and rating into local pack rankings. More reviews = better visibility.

Higher Conversion Rate

A practice with 200 reviews at 4.8 stars converts website visitors at a significantly higher rate than one with 20 reviews.

AI Search Signals

Review volume and rating are signals that AI tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity use when recommending local businesses — another AEO benefit.
