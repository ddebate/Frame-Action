The Shift in Search

The Way Clients Find Med Spas Has Changed

For years, Google's ten blue links were the gateway to new clients. That's changing fast. Today, a growing percentage of people are asking questions — and getting direct answers — from AI tools, never clicking a website at all.

When someone types "best Botox near me" into ChatGPT or asks Perplexity "what Med Spas are highest-rated in [city]?" — they get a curated answer, often citing specific practices by name. The practices that show up are the ones that have been optimized for AI. The ones that don't are invisible, even if they've invested years in traditional SEO.

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the discipline of making your Med Spa discoverable and citable by AI-powered tools. It's the next frontier of local search — and the window to be an early mover is right now.

How AEO Works

What Frame Action Does to Get You Cited by AI

AEO isn't a single tactic — it's a set of technical and content practices that make your practice readable, trustworthy, and citable by AI models. Here's exactly what we implement.

1

llms.txt Implementation

We create and maintain a structured llms.txt file on your website — the emerging standard that gives AI tools a direct, structured read of your practice: services offered, credentials, location, hours, and contact information. Think of it as your practice's business card for AI models, following the llmstxt.org specification.

2

Structured Data & Schema Markup

We implement comprehensive JSON-LD structured data including MedicalBusiness, LocalBusiness, FAQPage, and Service schemas. This machine-readable data helps AI tools understand your practice with precision — not guesswork.

3

FAQ Content Optimization

We write and structure FAQ content — "What is Botox?", "How long does filler last?", "What should I expect during a laser treatment?" — formatted so AI tools can directly cite your practice when answering these questions. This is one of the highest-leverage AEO tactics for Med Spas.

4

Content Architecture for AI Readability

AI models reward clear, authoritative, well-organized content. We audit and restructure your website content to use plain-language service descriptions, clear headings, concise answers to patient questions, and consistent entity language (your practice name, location, treatments) that AI can match to queries.

5

Citation Building & Authority Signals

AI tools pull from multiple sources — your website, Google Business Profile, Yelp, Healthgrades, and news mentions. We ensure your practice has consistent, authoritative citations across these sources, creating multiple pathways for AI tools to discover and recommend you.

6

Ongoing AEO Monitoring

We monitor your practice's appearances in AI-generated results over time, testing queries relevant to your treatments and location. As AI platforms evolve, our AEO strategy evolves with them — ensuring you maintain your visibility advantage as this space matures.

AEO vs. SEO

Why You Need Both — and Why AEO Can't Wait

Traditional SEO and AEO serve different search behaviors. A complete Med Spa marketing strategy requires both.

Traditional SEO

Optimizes for ranked blue links

  • → User searches Google, sees a list of links, clicks through
  • → Success = ranking in top 3 positions for key terms
  • → Well-established, mature best practices
  • → Competitive — most Med Spas are already attempting it

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

Optimizes for AI-generated answers

  • → User asks an AI assistant; AI answers directly, citing sources
  • → Success = being cited or recommended in AI responses
  • → Emerging — best practices still forming, early movers win
  • → Greenfield for Med Spas — almost no competition yet
The Early Mover Advantage is Real

AI search is growing rapidly, but most Med Spas — and most marketing agencies — aren't optimizing for it yet. The practices that build AEO authority now will have a significant advantage as this channel matures. Waiting means ceding ground to whoever acts first in your local market.

Common Questions

AEO FAQs

What is an llms.txt file?

An llms.txt file is a structured plain-text document placed at your website's root that gives AI language models a clear, organized summary of your practice — services, location, credentials, and contact info. It's similar in concept to robots.txt, but designed for AI models rather than search crawlers. The standard is defined at llmstxt.org.

Does my Med Spa need AEO if I'm already doing SEO?

Yes. SEO and AEO serve different search behaviors and different client journeys. Traditional SEO gets you found in Google's blue links. AEO gets you recommended when a potential client asks an AI assistant for help. Both channels are growing, and ignoring AEO means ceding ground in a rapidly expanding discovery channel.

How quickly does AEO produce results?

AEO results develop over weeks to months as AI models index your content and build trust signals. Technical implementations (llms.txt, schema) take effect quickly, while content authority builds over time. Unlike paid ads, AEO results compound — the earlier you start, the stronger your position becomes.
