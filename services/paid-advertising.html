What We Manage

Paid Channels That Work for Med Spas

Not all paid advertising channels are equal for aesthetic practices. We focus on the platforms that drive the highest-intent leads for Med Spa treatments.

Google Search Ads

Appear at the top of search results for "Botox near me," "laser treatments [city]," and every high-intent treatment query in your area. Capture clients at the moment they're ready to book.

Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

Visually-driven campaigns on the platforms where your target demographic spends time. Treatment showcases, before/after creative, and awareness campaigns that build local brand recognition.

Retargeting Campaigns

Re-engage visitors who browsed your website without booking. Treatment-specific retargeting ads follow potential clients with relevant messaging until they're ready to commit.

How We're Different

Med Spa-Specific Ad Strategy

Generic ad agencies apply the same playbook to every client. Frame Action's ad strategy is built around the specific dynamics of Med Spa client acquisition — high-value treatments, visual-first creative, and local intent signals.

Treatment-Level Campaign Structure

Separate campaigns for Botox, fillers, laser, body contouring — each with tailored keywords, ad copy, and landing pages.

Seasonal & Promotional Timing

Campaigns timed around Med Spa seasonal demand (holidays, summer, wedding season) and your specific promotions.

Transparent Monthly Reporting

Clear, plain-English monthly reports showing cost per lead, bookings generated, and ROI relative to ad spend and retainer.
