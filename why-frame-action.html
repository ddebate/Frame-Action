The Proven Model

Built on What Already Works

Frame Action applies the DentalQore model to Med Spas.

DentalQore is the leading dental marketing agency — built on the insight that niche-specific marketing, done right, dramatically outperforms generalist agencies for healthcare-adjacent practices. They pioneered deep vertical focus, rapid deployment, and results-oriented reporting for dental practices. It works.

Frame Action was built in collaboration with the DentalQore founders — taking their proven model and applying it to the Med Spa vertical, with one additional layer: Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) built in from day one, preparing Med Spa practices for the AI-powered future of search.

This isn't a startup experiment. It's a proven playbook applied to a new vertical, with the people who pioneered it involved in the process.
