Common Questions About Pricing

Pricing FAQs

Do I need to buy all six services?

No. We offer both bundled packages and individual services. Most clients start with a core bundle (website + SEO + AEO) and add services over time as they see results. We'll recommend the right starting point based on your current situation and goals.

What is the minimum commitment?

We're still working out the details of contract terms, but our philosophy is that we should earn your business every month. We'll share specifics when we talk — book a demo and ask us directly.

Are there setup fees?

Yes — for services that involve significant upfront work (website build, initial SEO audit and setup, AEO implementation), there's a one-time setup fee. Ongoing monthly management is then billed at a retainer rate. This model keeps the monthly cost lower while fairly compensating for launch-phase work.

How does Frame Action pricing compare to competitors?

We're designed to be meaningfully more affordable than enterprise platforms like Podium, while delivering more than generalist agencies at the same price point. Our AI-assisted workflows let us operate more efficiently — and we pass that savings on to clients. The goal is to be a no-brainer investment, not a premium luxury.
